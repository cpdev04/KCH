The Best Cancer Hospital in Bangalore

At Karnataka Cancer Hospital, we know how overwhelming a cancer diagnosis can be. That’s why we’re committed to providing advanced, compassionate, and patient-focused care. Our main aim is to offer best cancer treatment in Bangalore. We are a 50-bedded, state-of-the-art facility located in Bengaluru North. Karnataka Cancer Hospital was founded by Dr. Yathish Kumar H. M, who is a well- renowned surgical oncologist with over 20 years of experience. His vision was to create a world-class facility that combines advanced technology with compassionate care, making it one of the best cancer hospitals in Bangalore.

Our advanced infrastructure includes cutting-edge technology like a Linear Accelerator (SIDDHARTH- II) for precise radiation therapy, robotic systems for enhanced surgical accuracy, and a wide range of modern diagnostic tools. Whether you’re seeking treatments for complex cancers or need supportive care for pre-existing conditions, our facility is equipped to meet your needs. From breast and colorectal cancer surgeries to advanced therapies like immunotherapy and targeted therapy, our specialists develop treatment plans tailored to each patient’s unique condition and preferences.

Karnataka Cancer Hospital isn’t just about treating cancer—it’s about caring for people. With 50 inpatient beds, a focus on patient comfort, and a dedicated team of nurses, technicians, and doctors, we strive to make every patient feel supported at every step. We offer rapid appointment scheduling and on-site consultations with some of the best cancer doctors in the region, ensuring timely and effective care. If you’re searching for top cancer specialists in Bangalore, our team is here to provide hope, healing, and expert guidance.

We’re Here For You
