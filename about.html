Welcome To Karnataka Cancer Hospital

Karnataka Cancer Hospital & Research Centre was founded by Dr. Yathish Kumar H.M, a dedicated Masters in Surgical Oncology, with a vision to make cancer care affordable and accessible for everyone. Based in Bangalore, our hospital is equipped with the latest technology, providing a wide range of treatments and therapies for cancer patients at every stage of their journey.

Our Founder, Dr. Yathish Kumar H.M

Dr. Yathish Kumar H.M is a highly respected oncologist with over 20 years of expertise in surgical oncology. He earned his MBBS from JJM Medical College, MS in General Surgery from NHL Medical College, and MCh in Surgical Oncology from Gujarat Cancer Research Institute. Founder of Karnataka Cancer Hospital & Research Centre and Oncologique, Dr. Yathish is committed to affordable, world-class cancer care and advancing oncology research. Renowned for performing complex surgeries and holding a Ph.D. in Molecular Oncology, his groundbreaking work has earned him awards like the Karnataka Bandhu Award and the Global Excellence Award in Oncology.

Our Vision and Commitment

At Karnataka Cancer Hospital, we are committed to simplifying cancer care while offering the most effective treatments possible. We aim to provide comprehensive, multidisciplinary care through a combination of surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and personalized support services.

Our hospital is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by experts who are not only skilled in treating cancer but also passionate about making a difference in the lives of our patients. Our vision is to improve the quality of life for cancer patients by providing compassionate, affordable, and cutting-edge treatment.

We are also actively involved in cancer research, seeking innovative treatments and therapies that can benefit our patients and contribute to the broader fight against cancer.

At Karnataka Cancer Hospital, we understand the emotional and physical challenges that come with a cancer diagnosis. We are here to support you at every step of your journey with the best medical care, expert advice, and compassionate support. Let us be part of your journey to better health.

We offer a full spectrum of cancer treatments, from early detection to advanced surgical interventions. Whether you're battling breast cancer, lung cancer, or any other type, we provide cutting-edge therapies to give you the best chance of recovery.
